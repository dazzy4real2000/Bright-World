About Bright World

Bright world lights is your one-stop lighting shop.
If you can’t find the lighting solutions you are looking for in our store then you probably won’t find them anywhere else.

Centrally located in the province of Lekki

The door of our online store is open 24/7 but our physical store is located in Belgium,
in the Province of Antwerp, in Heist-op-den-Berg.

The store of electricity specialist Paul De Meutter

dmlights is the online store of Elektriciteit Paul De Meutter CV, which specialises in electrical materials and appliances. We pride ourselves on our significant technical knowledge and in-house expertise about electricity and lighting.

First steps in Paul’s garage

The online store is a subsidiary of Paul De Meutter (plc), who also founded the company. Paul De Meutter established his company about thirty years ago in his own garage, much like Google, developing the company into what it has become today.

His son Jef DM took the store online

Ten years ago Paul’s son, Jef De Meutter, started to dream of an online store. After a lot of work his dream finally came true. Today the online shop generates about 70% of the overall turnover of Elektriciteit Paul De Meutter.

2013 - Over 40,000 different products online

Currently Bright World Light is one of the world’s biggest online lighting stores. With over 40,000 different products by 88 quality manufacturers dmlights is a big market player. The company was gradually developed thanks to Paul De Meutter’s extensive knowledge and expertise with Jef’s cleverness as a growth factor. dmlights focuses heavily on customer service, something which can make or break an (online) store. Currently the dmlights support team consists of eight experienced staffers who work tirelessly to ensure customers’ satisfaction on a daily basis. Last but not least we recently revamped our product pages, making them easier to navigate as well as adding larger photos and reviews.
